Cajun Airfryer Salmon

This air fryer salmon is full of flavor of spiciness coming from the cajun seasoning with a hint of sweetness leaving it moist and delicious.

With a simple and easy recipe only containing 4 ingredients, leaving it ready in under 10-12 minutes.

If using unsalted Cajun seasoning, you may want to season the fillets with salt before serving. I like to use a Misto sprayer with oil, but feel free to use regular cooking spray. For best results, use 1-inch-thick, center-cut fillets.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 1 Tablespoon Cajun Seasoning
  • 1 Tablespoon Brown Sugar
  • 2(6 ounce) Skin-On Salmon Fillets

Steps

  1. Preheat the airfryer to 390Â°F.
  2. Grease the air fryer basket with cooking spray.
  3. Mix together Cajun Seasoning and Brown Sugar in a small bowl untill combined; spread onto a plate
  4. Rinse and dry salmon fillets with a paper towel and mist the fillets with cooking spray.
  5. Press fillets, flesh-side down, into seasoning mixture. After Place coated fillets, skin-side down, into prepared air fryer basket.
  6. Mist fillets again with cooking spray and close the lid. Cook the fillets in the preheated air fryer for 8-10 minutes depending on prefrence.
  7. After 8 minutes, remove from air fryer and let it rest for 2 minutes before serving.
